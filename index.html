by Christopher Wendt | Jun 5, 2018

We believe in security so much that we are going to offer a free standard SSL ($150.00 value!) to every one of our clients as a sign of appreciation for your business.

What is an SSL? According to Comodo, “an SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) is a standard security protocol for establishing encrypted links between a web server and a browser in an online communication”. You know that a website is secured when you see the S appear after the “HTTP” in any URL. You can also tell if a website is secure by looking in the upper left of your Chrome browser right before the URL. If there is a green lock, it means that the website is secure.

Do I need an SSL for my website if I’m not selling anything? Many people think that you only need an SSL if you are taking payment information or you have an online store. However, this is quickly becoming no longer the case, and the industry is slowly moving away from this. Google is encouraging everyone to make sure that every page on their website is secured, regardless of whether you are selling things or not. They are giving ranking and SEO preferences to those who have every web page secured.
